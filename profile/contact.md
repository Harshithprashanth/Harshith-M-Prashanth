## 📋 Research Collaboration Guidelines

If you are interested in collaborating on research, please reach out with the following information:

1. **Your background**: Academic/industry affiliation, research focus, relevant publications
2. **Collaboration type**: Joint paper, dataset contribution, code collaboration, or consultancy
3. **Specific project idea**: A brief description (3–5 sentences) of what you have in mind
4. **Timeline & commitment**: Expected duration and estimated time commitment
5. **Resources available**: Access to GPUs, datasets, domain expertise, or funding

I particularly welcome collaborations that:
- Span disciplinary boundaries (e.g., ECE × LLM, signal processing × NLP)
- Commit to open science practices (code and data release)
- Have clear real-world applications in industry or healthcare
- Involve rigorous empirical evaluation with honest reporting of results
